Vision

Our vision is to help the world’s population become the best holistic learners they can be and thus create a better world for all

Mission Statement

DRL is an organisation that seeks to ignite educational transformation that will help young people and those involved with them become the best learners they can be. We aim to help all young people, educators and parents shift their focus towards developing the learner. We aim help to all school children, university undergraduates, and (???) become the best learners they can be.

What is a Real Learner?

What is a Real Learner?

A real learner is someone who has not only developed the attributes of a great learner, but also continues to do so, independently if necessary and collaboratively if possible.

Real learners are those people who recognise that they stand a better chance of leading a successful and fulfilling life if they are good learners than if they are not. They recognise also that by all of us becoming better learners, we increase the chances of our survival.

The development of the learner is ultimately a personal mission — however, although it can be an independent mission one young person follows, it can also be the mission of a group or class or school or education system or perhaps even everyone on the planet. Wouldn’t the world be a better place if we were all the best learners we could be?

DRL uses fifteen attributes that allow learners to make the most of their education and experiences throughout their lives. We believe that Real Learners are:
