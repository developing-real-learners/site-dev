What is a Real Learner?

The Learner Profile

At Developing Real Learners, we believe that young people should have more than just the opportunity to learn — they should have a rich variety of opportunities to develop the attributes of a good learner. We believe that the future of education lies in programmes which place the learner at the centre of the curriculum.

We have identified fifteen learner attributes that allow learners to make the most of their education and experiences throughout their lives. We believe that Real Learners are:

Robust

Collaborative

Open-Minded

Passionate

Principled

Proactive

Caring

Informed

Balanced

Curious Inquirers

Independent

Thinking

Reflective

Courageous

Communicators
